Surabaya, Indonesia

Surabaya, the capital of East Java, is Indonesia's second-largest city and a vibrant hub of commerce and culture. Known as the "City of Heroes" for its pivotal role in Indonesia's independence struggle.

From the bustling streets of Tunjungan to the serene waters of the Kalimas River, Surabaya offers a unique blend of traditional Javanese culture and metropolitan energy.

Home to incredible culinary experiences like rujak cingur, Surabaya is a food lover's paradise. The warmth of its people, known as "Arek Suroboyo," makes every corner of this city feel like home.
